About

Hi! My name is Emily Desmedt and I am the artist behind Em’s Creations. I am currently a Graphic Design Student at Algonquin College, and a mother to a 5 year-old son.

Since a young age I've been creative. As a child, I would paint landscapes using acrylics with my grandmother. After high school, I decided that I wanted to try and make a career with my art, so I started the Graphic Design course at Algonquin College.

My interests include video games, horror movies, Marvel, DC, and Star Wars. Some of my favourite artists are Vincent Van Gogh, Claude Monet, Junji Ito, Hayao Miyazaki, Rob Czar, and Eepmon.

Hope you enjoy my creations!
